---

The Personal Data Inventory (PDI) is the standard biblical counseling intake form. Soul Care Manager includes a four-page PDI out of the box so you can start sending intake paperwork immediately without building anything from scratch.

## What's in the default PDI

The default PDI covers four pages:

| Page | Contents |
|---|---|
| **Personal & Contact** | Name, address, phone, email, date of birth, marital status, children, employer |
| **Spiritual Background** | Church membership, pastor's name, salvation and baptism history, spiritual disciplines |
| **Presenting Concerns & Goals** | What brought them to counseling, prior counseling history, medical information, current medications |
| **Consent** | Consent language and signature field |

## Sending the PDI

The simplest way to get the PDI to a new counselee is through the intake invite flow. See [Adding a Counselee](/counselees/adding-a-counselee) for the full steps.

If your center has **Auto-send PDI** enabled, a PDI link goes out automatically every time a new counselee is created — no extra steps needed. Your center admin can configure this under Center Settings.

## How PDI responses populate the counselee profile

Fields on the PDI can be mapped to fields on the counselee's profile. When the counselee submits the form, those fields are automatically filled in on their record — you don't have to copy anything over manually.

Mapped fields include: phone number, address, date of birth, marital status, number of children, church name, and pastor's name.

## Customizing the PDI

The default PDI is a starting point. You can edit it to add, remove, or rearrange fields to match how your practice collects intake information.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Forms">
    Click **Forms** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the default PDI">
    Find the PDI template marked as your default and open it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit the form">
    Use the drag-and-drop builder to modify pages and fields.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Changes apply to all future PDI links. Previously sent links are unaffected.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Each center has one default PDI. If you need a different intake form for a specific situation, you can create additional form templates and send them manually. See [Building a Custom Form](/forms/building-a-custom-form).
</Note>

## PDI link expiry

PDI links expire after 14 days for security. If a counselee hasn't completed the form in time, you can resend a fresh link from their record.
