---
title: "Reviewing Submissions"
description: "Read, highlight, annotate, and share counselee form submissions."
---

When a counselee submits a form, their responses appear in Soul Care Manager for you to review. You can highlight passages, add notes, correct answers, and share the submission with another counselor.

## Open a submission

From the counselee's record, go to their **Forms** tab and click any submitted form to open the full submission view.

## Highlighting responses

You can highlight any portion of a counselee's responses in one of several colors, similar to highlighting a physical document.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select the text">
    Click and drag to select a passage in the counselee's response.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a color">
    A color picker appears. Select the highlight color you want.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a note (optional)">
    You can attach a short note to any highlight — a reminder, a follow-up question, or an observation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Highlights and notes are private to you (and any counselors the submission is shared with). The counselee never sees them.

## Editing an answer

If a counselee mistyped something or gave an unclear answer that you want to correct for the record, you can edit the answer text directly on the submission.

Click the field you want to edit and look for the edit option. Your corrected version replaces the original on the submission record. The original text is not preserved, so use this only for genuine corrections.

## Sharing a submission with another counselor

You can share a submission read-only with another counselor at your center — for example, when seeking input on a case or handing off a counselee.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the submission">
    Navigate to the submission from the counselee's Forms tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Share">
    Find the Share option in the submission toolbar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the counselor">
    Choose a counselor from your center to share with. Add an optional note.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share">
    The counselor receives access to view the submission. They can see highlights and notes but cannot edit anything.
  </Step>
</Steps>

To revoke access, return to the sharing settings and remove the counselor.

## Marking a submission as reviewed

Once you've read through a submission, mark it as **Reviewed** to update its status. This helps you track which submissions still need attention.

Open the submission and use the **Mark as Reviewed** option at the top of the page.
