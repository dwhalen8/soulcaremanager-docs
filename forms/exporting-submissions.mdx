---
title: "Exporting Submissions as PDF"
description: "Download a formatted PDF of any form submission."
---

Any submitted form can be exported as a PDF — useful for printing, filing, or sharing outside the system.

## Export a submission

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the submission">
    Go to the counselee's **Forms** tab and click the submission you want to export.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Export PDF">
    Find the **Export PDF** button in the submission toolbar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download">
    The PDF is generated and downloads to your computer automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What's included in the PDF

The exported PDF includes:

- Your center's name and branding
- The counselee's name and submission date
- All form pages and responses, formatted for readability
- Your highlights and counselor notes (if any)

## Automatic PDF on submission

When a counselee submits a form, Soul Care Manager automatically generates a PDF and emails it to your center's admin notification addresses. This means you have a copy in your inbox as soon as the form comes in — you don't have to log in to retrieve it.

<Note>
  The automatic email PDF reflects the submission as received. If you later edit any answers in the submission, the stored PDF is not updated — you'll need to export a fresh one.
</Note>

## Printing

To print a submission, export it as a PDF first and print from your PDF viewer. Direct browser printing of the submission view is not recommended as it may not format correctly.
