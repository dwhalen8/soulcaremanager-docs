---
title: "Sending a Form"
description: "Send a form link to a counselee for them to complete online."
---

Forms are sent to counselees as a link they can open in any web browser — no account required on their end. They can complete the form on their phone, tablet, or computer.

## Send a form to a counselee

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the counselee's record">
    Click the counselee's name from your list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to their Forms tab">
    Find the Forms tab on their record.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Send Form">
    Select which form to send from your list of templates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send">
    An email goes out to the counselee with a link to complete the form. A submission record is created on their profile in draft status.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What the counselee sees

The counselee receives an email with a link to their form. Clicking it opens the form in their browser — no login required. They can fill it out at their own pace and submit when ready.

If the form has multiple pages, they'll see a progress indicator and can move forward and backward between pages before submitting.

## Form link expiry

Form links expire after **14 days** for security. If a counselee hasn't completed the form by then, you can resend a fresh link from their Forms tab.

<Note>
  Resending creates a new link and a new submission record. The expired link stops working immediately.
</Note>

## Auto-send PDI

If your center has **Auto-send PDI** enabled, a PDI link is sent automatically when a new counselee is created — you don't need to send it manually. Your center admin configures this under Center Settings.

## Require payment before submission

If your center charges for intake, you can require payment before a form can be submitted. This is configured on the form template itself under form settings. When enabled, the counselee is prompted to pay via Stripe before they can complete and submit the form.

## Tracking submission status

From the counselee's Forms tab you can see the status of every form you've sent:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Draft** | Link sent; counselee hasn't submitted yet |
| **Submitted** | Counselee has completed and submitted the form |
| **Reviewed** | You've reviewed the submission |

You'll also receive an email notification when a counselee submits a form.
