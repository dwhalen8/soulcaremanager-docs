---
title: "Adding a Counselee"
description: "How to create a new counselee record in Soul Care Manager."
---

There are three ways a counselee can enter the system: you add them manually, they submit a public intake form, or they come in as a prospective counselee through the public scheduling page.

## Add a counselee manually

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Counselees">
    Click **Counselees** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Add Counselee">
    Use the button in the top right corner of the counselee list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in their details">
    At minimum you'll need a first and last name. You can also add contact information, address, marital status, number of children, emergency contact, church, and pastor's name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** to create the record. The counselee is added as active and assigned to you.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Add via intake invite

If you want to send someone a PDI before their first session, use the intake invite flow instead of creating the record manually.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Counselees">
    Click **Counselees** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Send Intake Invite">
    Enter the prospective counselee's name and email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the invite">
    Soul Care Manager creates a prospective counselee record and emails them a link to complete the PDI. Once they submit it, their profile is automatically populated with their responses.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  If your center has **Auto-send PDI** enabled, a PDI link is automatically emailed to every new counselee you add manually — no extra steps needed.
</Tip>

## What gets created automatically

When a new counselee is added (manually or via intake), Soul Care Manager can:

- Create a prospective record with their name and email
- Send them a PDI link (if auto-send is on)
- Auto-populate their profile fields when they submit the form (phone, address, church, pastor, etc.)

## Profile photo

You can upload a profile photo for any counselee to make them easier to recognize at a glance. From their record, look for the avatar area near their name and click to upload. Photos are private — they are not visible to the counselee.

## Permanent delete

Archived counselees can be permanently deleted by center admins. This is gated behind an explicit confirmation step and is intended only for duplicate or test records. All other counselees should be archived rather than deleted so their history is preserved.
