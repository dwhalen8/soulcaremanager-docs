---
title: "Lending Books"
description: "Lend a book to a counselee and track when it's due back."
---

When you lend a book from your center's library, Soul Care Manager records who has it, when it's due, and keeps a full loan history per book and per counselee.

## Lend a book

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Book Library">
    Click **Book Library** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the book">
    Find the title you want to lend and click it to open the book record.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Lend">
    Use the **Lend** button to open the loan form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the borrower">
    Choose a counselee from your list, or enter a borrower name manually if lending to someone who isn't in the system.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the due date">
    Enter when you expect the book back.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    The loan is recorded and the available copy count for that title decrements by one.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  If lending this copy would bring your available copies below the minimum threshold you've set, you'll see a warning. You can proceed anyway — the warning is a reminder, not a block.
</Note>

## Borrower name snapshots

The borrower's name is saved on the loan record at the time of lending. This means the loan history stays accurate even if a counselee is later archived or their name changes. The historical record always shows who borrowed the book.

## Returning a book

When a counselee returns a book:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the book record">
    Find the title in your library and open it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the active loan">
    The current loans for that title are listed on the book record.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mark as returned">
    Click **Return** on the loan. The copy count increments back up and the loan is closed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Loan history

Every book has a full loan history showing every counselee who has borrowed it and when. Every counselee record also shows their borrowing history — which books they've had and when they returned them.

This means you always have a clear picture of where each copy has been.

## Overdue loans

Soul Care Manager tracks due dates on all active loans. Books past their due date are flagged so you can follow up with the borrower.

## Who can lend books

Any counselor can lend books from the center's library. The library is shared across the center — all counselors see the same inventory and loan records.
