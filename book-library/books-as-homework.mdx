---
title: "Books as Homework"
description: "Link physical books to homework assignments so loans open automatically when homework is assigned."
---

A homework resource in your library can be linked to a physical book from your center's collection. When that homework is assigned to a counselee, Soul Care Manager can automatically open a loan for the linked book to the same counselee — saving you the step of doing it separately.

## How it works

When a homework resource has a linked book:

1. You assign the homework to a counselee
2. Soul Care Manager opens a loan for the linked book automatically
3. The loan and the homework assignment are connected — they stay in sync

This is particularly useful for lessons that pair a teaching resource with a book the counselee reads alongside it.

## Link a book to a homework resource

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the homework resource">
    Go to **Resources** and open the homework item you want to link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the Book Link setting">
    Look for the **Linked Book** field in the resource settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search your library">
    Type to search your book inventory and select the title to link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    The book is now linked. Future assignments of this homework will automatically open a loan.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Keeping the loan and homework in sync

Once a loan and homework assignment are linked:

- **Marking the loan returned** also marks the homework complete
- **Marking the homework complete** also marks the loan returned

You only have to update one — the other follows automatically.

## What if the book isn't available?

If all copies of the linked book are currently on loan when you assign the homework, Soul Care Manager will warn you that no copies are available. You can still assign the homework — the loan just won't open automatically. You can open the loan manually once a copy comes back.

## Assignments without a linked book

Not all homework needs a linked book. The link is optional — most homework resources work perfectly well on their own. Only add a book link when the physical book is genuinely part of the assignment.
