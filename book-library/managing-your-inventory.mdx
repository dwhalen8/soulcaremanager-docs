title: "Managing Your Inventory"
description: "Track the physical books your center owns and lends to counselees."
---

The book library lets you manage the actual books on your counseling center's shelves — not digital resources, but physical copies you lend out to counselees. You can track how many copies you own, who has them, and when they're due back.

## Adding a book to your inventory

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Book Library">
    Click **Book Library** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search for the book">
    Use the search bar to find the title by name, author, or ISBN. Soul Care Manager searches Google Books and returns matching results with cover images and metadata.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the book">
    Click the title you want to add. The cover image, author, and description are pulled in automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your inventory details">
    Enter how many copies you own and set a minimum copies threshold (see below).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a category">
    Assign the book to a category: Counseling, Marriage & Family, Anxiety, Depression & Grief, Anger, Addiction, Identity & Shame, Spiritual Growth, Suffering, Sexuality, Parenting, or General.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    The book is added to your library inventory.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Book cover images

Cover images are pulled from Google Books when you add a title and stored locally in Soul Care Manager. This means the cover won't disappear if the Google Books link changes — your library entry is self-contained.

## Total copies and minimum threshold

Each book has two inventory numbers:

| Field | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Total copies** | How many copies your center currently owns |
| **Minimum copies** | The lowest number of copies you want to keep available before lending triggers a warning |

When lending a book would bring your available copies below the minimum threshold, Soul Care Manager shows a warning. You can override it and proceed — the warning is a prompt, not a hard block.

## Gifting and write-offs

If you give a copy to a counselee permanently or a copy is lost and won't be returned:

- **Gift**: Use the gift option to permanently transfer a copy to a counselee. The copy is removed from your inventory and a record of the gift is kept.
- **Write-off**: Use the write-off option to remove a lost or damaged copy from inventory.

Both actions decrement your total copies count. They cannot be undone, so use them only when the copy is genuinely leaving your collection.

## The premade book catalog

Soul Care Manager maintains a curated catalog of recommended biblical counseling titles. You can browse the catalog and add titles to your library with one click — the cover and metadata copy across automatically.

To browse the catalog, go to **Book Library** and look for the **Browse Catalog** tab.
