## Sign in

Go to [soulcaremanager.com/login](https://soulcaremanager.com/login) and enter your email and password.

<Note>
  Your account must be verified before you can log in. Check your inbox for a verification email when you first register — click the link inside to confirm your address.
</Note>

## First-time setup

If this is your first time logging in as a counselor or center admin, you'll be walked through a short onboarding wizard before reaching the main app. It covers:

- Your name and profile photo
- Your gender (used for ACBC supervision tracking)
- Your certification path and supervision status
- Notification preferences

You only need to complete this once. It takes about two minutes.

## Two-factor authentication (2FA)

Soul Care Manager supports two-factor authentication via an authenticator app (such as Google Authenticator or Authy). 2FA is optional but recommended for accounts that access sensitive counseling records.

To enable 2FA after logging in:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your profile">
    Click your name or avatar in the bottom of the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Security">
    Find the Two-Factor Authentication section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scan the QR code">
    Use your authenticator app to scan the code, then enter the six-digit code to confirm.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once enabled, you'll be asked for a code from your authenticator app each time you sign in.

## Forgot your password?

Click **Forgot password?** on the login page and enter your email address. You'll receive a reset link valid for one hour.

<Note>
  For security, the password reset page always says the email was sent — even if the address isn't in the system. This prevents anyone from guessing which email addresses have accounts.
</Note>

## Auto-logout

Each user can set an inactivity timeout that automatically signs them out after a period of no activity. This is recommended for shared computers. You can configure it in your profile settings.

## Trouble logging in?

If you're locked out or need help, contact your center administrator. If you're the center admin, reach out to us using the **Contact** link in the app sidebar.
