---
title: "What is Soul Care Manager?"
description: "A practice management system built specifically for biblical counselors."
---

Soul Care Manager is purpose-built for ACBC-certified biblical counselors and the centers where they serve. It handles the day-to-day work of running a counseling practice — keeping track of counselees, scheduling sessions, taking notes, sending intake paperwork, assigning homework, and tracking supervision hours — with the language and workflows of biblical counseling rather than clinical mental-health software.

## Who it's for

Soul Care Manager is designed for:

- **Independent biblical counselors** managing their own caseload
- **Counseling centers** with multiple counselors working under one roof
- **Counselors in ACBC certification** who need to track supervision hours and session counts

## How access works

Soul Care Manager uses invitation codes to add new users. You won't be able to create an account without one. If you're joining an existing counseling center, your center administrator will send you an invite. If you're starting a new practice, contact us to get set up.
