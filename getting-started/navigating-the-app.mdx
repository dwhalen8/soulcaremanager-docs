## The sidebar

The sidebar on the left is your main navigation. What you see depends on your role — counselors see their own caseload and schedule, while center admins see additional tools for managing the practice.

The main sections are:

| Section | What's there |
|---|---|
| **Dashboard** | A summary of upcoming appointments, recent activity, and announcements |
| **Counselees** | Your full caseload — add, search, and open individual records |
| **Schedule** | Your calendar, appointments, and time-off blocks |
| **Sessions** | A list of all sessions, past and upcoming |
| **Notes** | A journal of notes and completed sessions across all counselees |
| **Forms** | Intake forms and submissions |
| **Resources** | Your resource and homework library |
| **Book Library** | Physical books your center lends to counselees |
| **Settings** | Your profile, notification preferences, and center settings (admins) |

## Opening a counselee record

Click any counselee's name from the Counselees list to open their record. From there you can view their profile, appointments, notes, forms, files, supervision logs, and more — all in one place organized by tabs.

## Switching roles

If your account has more than one role (for example, you are both a counselor and a center admin), you'll see a role switcher at the bottom of the sidebar. Clicking it lets you switch between roles without logging out.

Switching roles changes what you can see and do. If you're on the Schedule page as a counselor and switch to your admin role, you stay on the Schedule page — just with the broader admin view.

## The counselee portal

The counselee portal is a separate, simplified interface your counselees use to log in. It looks different from the main app by design — counselees only see their own appointments, homework, forms, and documents.

If your account includes a client role (for example, you're also receiving counseling), you can switch to the portal view the same way you switch any other role.

## Getting help

Every page in the app has a **Contact** option in the sidebar for reaching the platform team, and a **Report a Bug / Request a Feature** option that goes directly to the development queue.
