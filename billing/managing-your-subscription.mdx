---
title: "Managing Your Subscription"
description: "Start, change, or cancel your Soul Care Manager subscription."
---

Subscription management is handled by center admins through Stripe's customer portal. You don't need to contact us to make most changes — the portal gives you direct control over your plan, payment method, and billing history.

## Start a subscription

If you're on a free trial or your subscription has lapsed:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Billing">
    Click **Settings** in the sidebar, then **Billing**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a billing interval">
    Select monthly or annual billing. Current pricing for both options is shown on the page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Subscribe">
    You'll be taken to Stripe's checkout page to enter your payment details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete checkout">
    Once payment is confirmed, your subscription is active immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Change your payment method

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Billing">
    Click **Settings** in the sidebar, then **Billing**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Manage Subscription">
    This opens the Stripe customer portal in a new tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update payment method">
    Add a new card or update your existing one from the portal.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Switch between monthly and annual billing

You can switch billing intervals from the Stripe customer portal. Changes take effect at the end of your current billing period.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Billing → Manage Subscription">
    This opens the Stripe customer portal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update your plan">
    Select the interval you want to switch to.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## View invoices and billing history

All invoices are available in the Stripe customer portal under **Billing History**. You can download individual invoices as PDFs for your records.

## Cancel your subscription

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Billing → Manage Subscription">
    This opens the Stripe customer portal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Cancel subscription">
    Select the option to cancel. Stripe will confirm the cancellation and tell you when your access ends.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period — you won't lose access immediately. Your data remains intact for a period after cancellation in case you reactivate.
</Note>

## Reactivate a canceled subscription

Go to **Settings → Billing** and click **Subscribe** to start a new subscription. Your existing data — counselee records, sessions, notes, and files — will still be there.
