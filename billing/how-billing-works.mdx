---
title: "How Billing Works"
description: "Soul Care Manager is billed per active counselor seat on a monthly or annual basis."
---

Soul Care Manager uses per-seat billing through Stripe. Each active counselor or center admin at your practice counts as one seat, and you're billed based on how many seats you have.

## What counts as a seat

A seat is any active user with the **counselor** or **center admin** role. Other roles — intake staff and counselees — do not count toward your seat total.

Seats are counted automatically. When you add a new counselor or admin, your subscription quantity updates at the next billing cycle. When someone is deactivated, that seat is released.

## Billing intervals

You can choose to be billed **monthly** or **annually**. Annual billing is available at a discounted rate compared to paying month to month.

To see current pricing, go to **Settings → Billing** in the app. Pricing is shown for both intervals so you can compare before subscribing.

## Subscription states

| State | What it means |
|---|---|
| **Active** | Subscription is current; full access to all features |
| **Trialing** | In a free trial period; full access |
| **Past due** | A payment failed; a banner appears in the app but access continues temporarily |
| **Canceled** | Subscription has ended; access is restricted |

If your subscription lapses, you'll see a notification in the app with instructions for how to reactivate.

## Free access accounts

Some centers — ministry partners, comped accounts, or centers in special arrangements — are marked as free access by the Soul Care Manager team. Free access accounts bypass subscription gating entirely and don't need an active Stripe subscription.

If you believe your center qualifies, contact us through the app.

## Taxes and invoices

Stripe handles tax calculation and invoicing. Invoices are available through the Stripe customer portal, which you can access from **Settings → Billing → Manage Subscription**.
