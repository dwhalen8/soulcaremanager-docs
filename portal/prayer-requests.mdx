---
title: "Prayer Requests"
description: "Counselees can submit prayer requests through the portal, and counselors can track and respond to them."
---

The prayer request feature gives counselees a simple way to share ongoing prayer needs with their counselor between sessions. Requests are submitted through the counselee portal and appear on the counselor's end for follow-up.

## How counselees submit a prayer request

From the portal, counselees go to the **Prayer Requests** section and click **New Request**. They write out their request and submit it. There's no character limit — they can share as much or as little as they want.

Submitted requests are sent directly to their counselor and visible in the counselor app.

## Viewing prayer requests as a counselor

Prayer requests from a counselee appear on their record in the counselor app.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the counselee's record">
    Click their name from your counselee list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Prayer Requests">
    Look for the Prayer Requests section on their record.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review requests">
    Open any request to read the full text.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Request statuses

You can update the status of each prayer request as you engage with it:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Open** | Active prayer need; not yet resolved |
| **Praying** | You've acknowledged the request and are actively praying |
| **Answered** | The prayer has been answered; mark it as such to close the loop |
| **Archived** | No longer active; kept for reference |

Updating the status helps you keep track of what needs attention and gives you a record of how God has worked over the course of the counseling relationship.

## Privacy

Prayer requests are visible only to the counselee who submitted them and their assigned counselor. Center admins do not have access to prayer request content by default.
