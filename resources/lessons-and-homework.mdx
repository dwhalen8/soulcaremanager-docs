title: "Lessons & Default Homework"
description: "Create lessons that automatically assign bundled homework when used in a session."
---

Lessons are a special resource category designed for structured teaching content you work through with a counselee during session. What makes lessons distinct is the ability to bundle default homework with them — when you assign a lesson, the associated homework assignments go out automatically.

## What makes a lesson different

A lesson typically contains teaching content you'd present or read through together during a session. Paired with default homework, it creates a complete unit: you teach the material in session, and the counselee receives the follow-up homework automatically.

This removes the manual step of remembering to assign related homework every time you use a particular lesson.

## Create a lesson

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Resources">
    Click **Resources** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click New Resource">
    Choose **Lesson** as the category.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the lesson content">
    Write the teaching content using the rich-text editor. Attach any relevant files.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add default homework">
    In the lesson settings, find the **Default Homework** section. Search your library and select the homework resources that should go out automatically when this lesson is assigned.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    The lesson is ready to assign from the Session View or a counselee's record.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Assigning a lesson

Assign a lesson the same way you'd assign any resource — from the Session View or from the counselee's Resources tab. When you assign the lesson:

1. The lesson itself is assigned to the counselee
2. All default homework linked to the lesson is assigned simultaneously
3. The counselee receives emails for both the lesson and the homework

You don't need to assign the homework separately.

## Linking a book to a lesson

If a lesson is associated with a physical book from your center's library, you can link the book to the lesson. When the lesson is assigned, Soul Care Manager can automatically open a loan for that book to the same counselee.

See [Books as Homework](/book-library/books-as-homework) for how the loan and homework assignment stay in sync.

## Modifying default homework for a specific counselee

If you want to assign a lesson but skip one of the default homework items — or add something extra — you can do so after assigning. Go to the counselee's Resources tab and add or remove assignments individually. The defaults are a starting point, not a requirement.
