---
title: "Assigning Resources"
description: "Assign homework and reading material to a counselee from your resource library."
---

Assigning a resource makes it visible to the counselee in their portal and sends them an email with the content. You can assign from the resource library, from the Session View, or directly from a counselee's record.

## Assign from the Session View

The most common workflow is assigning homework at the end of a session.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the session">
    Navigate to the Session View for the current appointment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the homework area">
    Look for the homework or resources module in the Session View.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search your library">
    Type to search for the resource you want to assign.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign it">
    Click **Assign**. The resource is added to the counselee's homework list immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Assign from the counselee's record

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the counselee's record">
    Click their name from your counselee list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to the Resources tab">
    Find the Resources or Homework tab on their record.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Assign Resource">
    Search your library and select the resource to assign.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What the counselee receives

When you assign a resource, the counselee receives an email containing:

- The resource title and content in the body of the email
- Any files attached to the resource
- Your center's logo and branding (if configured)

The resource also appears in the counselee's portal under their homework list.

## Personalize before assigning

If you want to tailor a resource for a specific counselee without changing the library version, copy it first and edit the copy. Then assign the personalized copy to that counselee. Your original stays unchanged in the library.

## Tracking completion

From the counselee's Resources tab, you can see the status of each assigned resource:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Assigned** | Sent to the counselee; not yet started |
| **In Progress** | Counselee has marked it as started |
| **Completed** | Counselee has marked it done, or you've marked it done on their behalf |

You can mark a resource complete from the counselor side as well — useful if you discussed it in session and want to update the record without waiting for the counselee to do it in the portal.

## Carry-over homework

Open homework from previous sessions surfaces automatically in the next session's homework review module. This makes it easy to follow up on what was assigned without digging through old session notes. See [Session Modules](/sessions/session-modules) for more.
