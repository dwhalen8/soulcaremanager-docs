## Create an appointment

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your Schedule">
    Click **Schedule** in the sidebar and navigate to the date you want.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click to add an appointment">
    Click the **New Appointment** button or click directly on a time slot in the calendar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the details">
    Select the counselee, set the start and end time, choose a session type, and add a location if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    The appointment appears on your calendar immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Appointment details

| Field | Notes |
|---|---|
| **Counselee** | Required. Start typing to search your caseload. |
| **Session type** | The kind of session (initial, follow-up, etc.) |
| **Location** | Optional. Recent locations are suggested as you type. |
| **Additional counselees** | For joint sessions with couples or families — add more than one counselee to a single appointment. |
| **Paid** | Mark whether the session has been paid for. |
| **Private** | Private appointments are hidden from other counselors when your calendar is shared. |

## Recurring appointments

To set up a repeating appointment:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create the appointment">
    Fill in the details as normal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable recurrence">
    Toggle on **Recurring** and choose the frequency: weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set an end date">
    Choose when the recurrence should stop.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Soul Care Manager creates all the future occurrences at once.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Editing a recurring appointment

When you edit or cancel a recurring appointment, you'll be asked whether you want to change just this one occurrence or all future occurrences from this point forward.

- **This occurrence only** — changes just the selected date; the rest of the series is untouched
- **This and all future occurrences** — updates every upcoming appointment in the series

## Cancelling an appointment

To cancel an appointment, open it from the calendar and select **Cancel**. You'll be prompted to enter a cancellation reason, which is saved on the appointment record and visible in the Session View.

<Note>
  Cancelled appointments are not deleted — they stay on the record so the history is complete. If you want to remove an appointment entirely, delete it instead of cancelling it.
</Note>

## Appointment reminders

Soul Care Manager can automatically email counselees a reminder before each session. See [Appointment Reminders](/scheduling/appointment-reminders) for how to configure them.
