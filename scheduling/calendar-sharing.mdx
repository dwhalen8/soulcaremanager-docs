## Sync to your calendar app

Soul Care Manager provides a private calendar feed you can subscribe to in Apple Calendar, Google Calendar, Outlook, or any calendar app that supports iCal.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to your Schedule">
    Click **Schedule** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find your calendar feed link">
    Look for the **Calendar Sync** or **iCal Feed** option. Copy the feed URL.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add it to your calendar app">
    In your calendar app, choose to subscribe to a calendar by URL and paste the feed link.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your Soul Care Manager appointments will appear in your external calendar app and update automatically as things change.

<Note>
  The iCal feed is read-only. You can view your appointments in Apple Calendar or Google Calendar, but changes must be made in Soul Care Manager — edits in your external app won't sync back.
</Note>

### Regenerating your feed link

If you need to revoke access to the feed — for example, if the link was shared unintentionally — you can regenerate it. The old link will stop working immediately. Go to your calendar sync settings and choose **Regenerate Link**.

## Share your calendar with another counselor

You can share your calendar with other counselors at your center. This is useful when scheduling joint sessions, checking for availability conflicts, or providing coverage.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Calendar Sharing settings">
    Go to **Schedule** and find the **Sharing** option.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose who to share with">
    Select a counselor from your center to share your calendar with.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set privacy options">
    Choose whether to show or hide your **private appointments** in the shared view. If you hide them, the counselor will see that time as blocked but won't see the appointment details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    The counselor will now see your calendar alongside their own.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Color coding

When more than one counselor's events are visible on the same calendar, each counselor's appointments appear in their own color. Each counselor can set their preferred color in their profile settings. A filter at the top of the calendar lets you show or hide individual counselors.
