---
title: "Follow-Ups"
description: "Capture action items at the end of a session and carry them into the next one."
---

Follow-ups are action items you capture at the end of a session — things to address, check on, or remember before the next meeting. They carry forward automatically so nothing gets lost between sessions.

## Adding a follow-up

At the end of a session, look for the **Follow-Ups** section in the Session View. Click **Add Follow-Up** and enter the item. You can add as many as you need.

Follow-ups are saved with the session record and automatically surface in the next session for that counselee.

## Follow-ups in the next session

When you open a new session for a counselee, any open follow-ups from previous sessions appear at the top of the Session View. You can:

- **Mark them complete** — removes them from the carry-forward list
- **Keep them open** — they continue to appear in future sessions until resolved
- **Add notes** — update a follow-up with what was discussed or decided

This means you never have to scroll back through old session notes to remember what you said you'd come back to.

## Follow-ups vs. homework

Follow-ups and homework serve different purposes:

| | Follow-Ups | Homework |
|---|---|---|
| **Who it's for** | The counselor — things to remember or address | The counselee — tasks for them to complete between sessions |
| **Where it appears** | In the counselor's Session View only | In the counselee's portal and on their assigned resources list |
| **How it's assigned** | Added during a session | Assigned from the resource library or created on the spot |

Use follow-ups for your own reminders. Use homework for assignments you're giving the counselee to work on.
