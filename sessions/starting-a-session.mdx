---
title: "Starting a Session"
description: "Open the Session View and begin documenting a counseling appointment."
---

The Session View is the workspace you'll have open during an actual counseling session. It brings together everything you need — a timer, your notes, homework, follow-ups, and the counselee's history — in one focused page.

## Open a session from the calendar

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to your Schedule">
    Click **Schedule** in the sidebar and find today's appointment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the appointment">
    Click the appointment to open its detail view.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start the session">
    Click **Start Session**. The Session View opens for that appointment.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Open a session from the Sessions list

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Sessions">
    Click **Sessions** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the session">
    Use the **Upcoming** tab to find today's session, or search by counselee name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open it">
    Click the session to open the Session View.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The session timer

A timer at the top of the Session View tracks how long the session runs. Click **Start** when the session begins and **Stop** when it ends. The elapsed time is saved with the session record.

The timer auto-saves as it runs — if your browser closes or your connection drops, the time already recorded is preserved.

## Auto-save

Everything you type in the Session View is saved automatically as you go. You don't need to click a save button. A small indicator shows when the last save occurred.

## Completing a session

When the session is over, click **Complete Session**. This marks the session as completed and locks the record. You can still view it afterward but editing is limited.

<Note>
  You don't have to complete a session immediately. If you want to finish your notes after the counselee has left, you can leave the session open and come back to it. Just make sure to complete it before the next session so your history stays clean.
</Note>

## Navigating back

Use the breadcrumb at the top of the page to return to the counselee's record or your calendar without losing your work. Everything is saved automatically.
