---
title: "Joint Sessions"
description: "Document sessions with couples, families, or multiple counselees in one shared workspace."
---

Joint sessions are for appointments that include more than one counselee — couples, families, or any situation where you're meeting with multiple people at once. Soul Care Manager gives joint sessions a shared workspace while keeping each counselee's notes, homework, and follow-ups separate.

## Setting up a joint appointment

When creating an appointment, add multiple counselees using the **Additional Counselees** field. Each person you add becomes part of the joint session.

All counselees in the appointment must already have records in your system. If someone is new, add their counselee record first.

## The joint session workspace

When you open a joint session, the layout is divided into two areas:

- **Shared area (left)**: a single timer, shared session status, and shared start/end timestamps that apply to the whole appointment
- **Per-counselee tabs (right)**: a tab for each counselee with their own notes, homework, and follow-ups

This means you run one timer for the whole session rather than separate timers per person, while still keeping each person's documentation distinct.

## Starting and stopping the shared timer

Click **Start** in the shared area to begin the session. The timer runs for all counselees simultaneously. Click **Stop** when the session ends. The elapsed time is recorded on the shared session record.

## Per-counselee notes and homework

Click each counselee's tab to switch between their individual documentation. Notes, homework assignments, and follow-ups entered on one tab belong to that counselee only — they don't appear on the other counselee's record.

## Sending joint session homework

At the end of a joint session, you can send a homework email to any or all of the counselees in one action.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the joint session">
    Navigate to the session from the calendar or Sessions list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Send Homework Email">
    Find the homework email option in the joint session toolbar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select recipients">
    Choose which counselees should receive the email. You can send to all or just some.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send">
    Each selected counselee receives an email with their assigned homework for the session.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Completing a joint session

Complete the session from the shared area. Completing it marks the session as done for all counselees in the appointment simultaneously.

<Note>
  Each counselee in a joint session also has their own individual session record visible on their personal counselee page. The shared timer and status are reflected on all of them.
</Note>
