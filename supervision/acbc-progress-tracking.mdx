---
title: "ACBC Progress Tracking"
description: "Track your progress toward ACBC certification or fellowship using Soul Care Manager."
---

Soul Care Manager tracks supervision and counseling hours in the format ACBC requires, with separate progress views for counselors in Initial certification and those pursuing Fellowship.

## Setting your supervision status

During the onboarding wizard, you'll be asked to select your ACBC supervision status. You can update it later from your profile settings.

| Status | Who it's for |
|---|---|
| **Initial** | Counselors working toward initial ACBC certification |
| **Fellowship** | Certified counselors working toward ACBC Fellowship |

Your progress dashboard adjusts automatically based on which status you've selected.

## Initial certification tracking

For counselors in the **Initial** phase, Soul Care Manager tracks:

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| **Supervisor hours** | Hours logged in supervision sessions |
| **Total counseling sessions** | Number of counseling sessions completed |
| **Sessions remaining** | How many more sessions to reach the 10-session threshold per case |

Your dashboard shows a running total of each metric so you can see at a glance where you stand.

## Fellowship tracking

For counselors pursuing **Fellowship**, Soul Care Manager tracks:

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| **Total supervision hours** | Cumulative hours spent in supervision |
| **Supervision sessions** | Number of supervision meetings held |
| **Total counseling hours** | Cumulative hours spent in counseling sessions |

A progress bar toward the 500-hour counseling goal is displayed on your dashboard.

<Note>
  Fellowship supervision tracking is available to male counselors only. This reflects the ACBC discipleship boundary that limits fellowship-status supervision to male counselors. Female counselors in Soul Care Manager are not shown fellowship supervision options.
</Note>

## Where hours come from

Supervision hours come from entries in your supervision logs. Counseling hours come from your completed session records — specifically the time tracked by the session timer.

For the most accurate progress tracking, make sure you:

- Log supervision entries promptly after each supervision meeting
- Start and stop the session timer during each counseling session

## Viewing your progress

Your supervision progress is visible on your counselor dashboard. You can also see a breakdown per counselee — how many sessions you've had with each person and how many supervision entries are tied to their case.
